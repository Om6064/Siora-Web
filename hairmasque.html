How to Use

  • Step 1: Cleanse Shampoo your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Step 2: Apply Take a generous amount of the masque and apply it evenly from mid-lengths to ends.
  • Step 3: Detangle Use fingers or a wide-tooth comb to spread the product and detangle hair.
  • Step 4: Wait Leave it on for 5–10 minutes to allow deep conditioning.
  • Step 5: Rinse Rinse thoroughly with cool or lukewarm water for best results.

Siora Silk Glow Body Oil

₹849 – ₹1049 (introductory)

Size: 100ml | 3.38 fl. oz

Siora Silk Glow Body Oil is a lightweight, non-greasy formula designed to deeply nourish, soften, and illuminate the skin with a radiant glow. Crafted with a blend of luxurious botanical oils, this body oil hydrates and enhances skin texture while leaving behind a delicate, soothing fragrance. Perfect for daily use or special occasions when you want your skin to shimmer with healthy, luminous beauty.

Key Ingredients & Their Role

Sweet Almond Oil

Deeply moisturizes and softens the skin, rich in Vitamin E.

Jojoba Oil

Balances skin’s natural oils and provides long-lasting hydration.

Rosehip Oil

Improves skin elasticity and reduces the appearance of scars.

Argan Oil

Nourishes dry skin and enhances natural glow.

Vitamin E

Acts as a powerful antioxidant, protecting skin from environmental damage

Chamomile Extract

Soothes irritation and calms the skin with anti-inflammatory properties.

Mica (Mineral Shimmer)

Adds a subtle, natural-looking glow to the skin without greasiness.

How to Use

  • Step 1: Dispense Pump a few drops of body oil into your palm.
  • Step 2: Warm Rub hands together to activate the oil.
  • Step 3: Apply Massage gently onto clean, damp skin using circular motions.
  • Step 4: Focus Give extra attention to dry areas like elbows, knees, and heels.
  • Step 5: Let It Absorb Wait a few minutes for full absorption before getting dressed.

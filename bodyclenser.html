Siora Refresh & Repair Body Cleanser

₹699 – ₹899 (introductory)

Size: 250ml | 8.45 fl. oz

A luxurious, sulfate-free body cleanser crafted to gently purify, deeply hydrate, and revive tired skin. Infused with skin-loving botanicals and mild surfactants, this cleanser delivers a spa-like experience while maintaining your skin's natural moisture balance. Perfect for daily use, it's designed to awaken the senses and leave your skin soft, smooth, and replenished.

Key Ingredients & Their Role

Aloe Vera Extract

Soothes, hydrates, and calms irritated skin

Niacinamide

Brightens skin tone and improves texture

Tea Tree Oil

Fights acne-causing bacteria and refreshes the skin

Coconut-Derived Surfactants

Gently cleanse without stripping natural oils

Panthenol (Vitamin B5)

Deeply moisturizes and strengthens skin barrier

Glycerin

Maintains skin moisture and prevents dryness

Chamomile Extract

Calms and refreshes the skin; ideal for sensitive types

Product Benefits

  • Deeply cleanses without over-drying
  • Soothes and calms irritated or sensitive skin
  • Supports skin repair and barrier health
  • Leaves skin feeling refreshed, soft, and smooth
  • Suitable for daily use and all skin types

Clean Beauty Promise

  • No parabens
  • No sulfates
  • No artificial fragrance
  • No mineral oil or silicones
  • Dermatologically tested
  • Cruelty-free & eco-conscious
  • Made with Ethically Sourced Natural Ingredients

How to Use

  • Step 1: Take a generous amount onto wet hands, loofah, or sponge
  • Step 2: Massage gently onto damp skin in circular motions to create a rich lather.
  • Step 3: Focus on rough or dull areas like elbows, knees, and back.
  • Step 4: Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  • Step 5: Pat dry and follow with body lotion or oil for added hydration.

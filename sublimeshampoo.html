    Siora Sublime Shine Shampoo

    ₹849 – ₹1049 (introductory)

    Size: 250ml

    Siora Sublime Shine Shampoo is a luxurious, sulfate-free formula designed to gently cleanse while restoring the hair’s natural shine and strength. Infused with nourishing botanicals and advanced actives, it revitalizes dull, tired strands, leaving your hair soft, silky, and luminous from root to tip. Crafted for all hair types and safe for color-treated hair, this shampoo brings salon-level brilliance with every wash.

    Key Ingredients & Their Role

    Argan Oil

    Deeply nourishes hair, adds shine, and reduces frizz

    Keratin Protein

    Strengthens hair strands, repairs damage, and smoothens texture

    Aloe Vera Extract

    Soothes the scalp, hydrates hair, and reduces irritation

    Vitamin B5 (Panthenol)

    Improves elasticity, retains moisture, and enhances shine

    Rice Water Ferment

    Promotes hair growth, increases shine, and improves hair texture

    Coconut-Derived Cleansers

    Gently cleanse the scalp without stripping natural oils

    Green Tea Extract

    Rich in antioxidants, supports scalp health, and reduces hair fall

    How to Use

    • Step 1: Wet Hair Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.
    • Step 2: Take Shampoo Dispense a small amount into your palm.
    • Step 3: Apply & Massage Apply to scalp and gently massage with fingertips.
    • Step 4: Rinse Off Rinse out all the shampoo with water.
    • Step 5: Repeat if Required Repeat only if your hair feels excessively oily or dirty.

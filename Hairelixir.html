Siora Intense Repair Hair Elixir

₹749 – ₹949 (introductory)

Size: 30ml | 1 fl. oz

Siora Intense Repair Hair Elixir is a luxurious, multi-functional hair treatment crafted to deeply nourish, strengthen, and repair damaged hair. Powered by a rich blend of botanical oils and active ingredients, this elixir helps reduce breakage, tame frizz, and restore silky softness — all without weighing the hair down. Ideal for all hair types, especially dry, brittle, and chemically treated hair.

Key Ingredients & Their Role

Argan Oil

Rich in Vitamin E and fatty acids, it deeply moisturizes, tames frizz, and adds shine.

Jojoba Oil

Balances scalp’s natural oils, strengthens hair from root to tip.

Rosemary Extract

Stimulates hair follicles, promotes growth, and improves scalp circulation.

Pea Protein

Helps repair damaged strands, adds strength and resilience.

Vitamin E

A powerful antioxidant that protects hair from environmental damage and UV rays.

Baobab Oil

Adds elasticity and softness, making hair more manageable and smooth.

Squalane

Seals in moisture without greasiness, improves texture, and adds lightweight hydration.

Product Benefits

  • Boosts shine and softness
  • Nourishes scalp

How to Use

  • Step 1: Dispense Take 2–3 drops of elixir in your palm (use more for longer hair).
  • Step 2: Warm It Up Rub between your palms to activate the oils.
  • Step 3: Apply to Scalp Massage gently into clean, dry or damp scalp using fingertips.
  • Step 4: Glide Through Hair Run hands through lengths and ends of hair, avoiding the roots if used as a styling serum.
  • Step 5: Leave In / Overnight Use as a leave-in treatment or keep overnight and wash next morning for intense repair.

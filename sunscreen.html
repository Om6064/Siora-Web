Siora Matte Defense Sunscreen SPF 50 PA+++

₹799 – ₹999 (introductory)

Size: 50g | 1.76 oz.

Siora Matte Defense Sunscreen is a lightweight, non-greasy daily protector formulated with SPF 50 PA+++ to shield your skin from harmful UVA and UVB rays. Designed with a matte finish, it controls excess oil, minimizes shine, and leaves no white cast — making it ideal for the Indian climate and all skin tones. Suitable for all genders and skin types, including sensitive skin.

Key Ingredients & Their Role

Zinc Oxide (Non-Nano)

Provides broad-spectrum UVA & UVB protection, gentle on sensitive skin

Titanium Dioxide

Offers physical sun protection and helps prevent sunburns

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

Evens skin tone, controls oil, and supports the skin barrier

Green Tea Extract

Rich in antioxidants, soothes skin, and helps reduce inflammation

Aloe Vera Extract

Hydrates and calms the skin, preventing irritation

Vitamin E

Protects against environmental damage and nourishes the skin

Matte Complex (Natural Silica)

Absorbs excess oil, blurs pores, and gives a smooth, matte finish

How to Use

  • Step 1: Cleanse Your Skin Start with a clean face by using your daily cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and impurities.
  • Step 2: Moisturize (if needed) If your skin feels dry, apply a lightweight moisturizer before sunscreen for added hydration.
  • Step 3: Apply Sunscreen Take a coin-sized amount of the Siora Matte Defense Sunscreen and dot it across your face and neck.
  • Step 4: Spread Evenly Gently massage the sunscreen into your skin using upward, circular motions until fully absorbed. Avoid rubbing harshly.
  • Step 5: Reapply as Needed Reapply every 2–3 hours, especially if you're outdoors, sweating, or after swimming, for continuous protection.

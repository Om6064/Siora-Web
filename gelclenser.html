Siora Crystal Clear Gel Cleanser

₹649 – ₹849 (introductory)

Size: 100ml | 3.4 oz.

A gentle yet powerful gel-based facial cleanser designed to purify, refresh, and restore the skin without stripping its natural moisture. Enriched with natural botanicals and soothing ingredients, it deeply cleanses pores, removes impurities, excess oil, and makeup residue — leaving the skin feeling soft, smooth, and radiantly clean. Ideal for daily use and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Key Ingredients & Their Role

Green Tea Extract

Rich in antioxidants; calms the skin, reduces inflammation & fights acne-causing bacteria.

Aloe Vera Juice

Soothes, hydrates, and heals irritated skin while maintaining moisture balance.

Willow Bark Extract

A natural source of salicylic acid; gently exfoliates and unclogs pores.

Vitamin B5 (Panthenol)

Improves skin hydration, softness & elasticity.

Cucumber Extract

Refreshes and cools the skin, reduces puffiness and redness.

Glycerin

Deeply hydrates without making the skin greasy.

How to Use

  • Step 1: Dispense Pump out a small amount of the gel cleanser (1–2 pumps) onto clean, dry hands.
  • Step 2: Activate Add a little water and gently rub your hands together to create a light foam or lather.
  • Step 3: Massage Apply to your damp face and neck. Massage in gentle, upward circular motions for about 30–60 seconds.
  • Step 4: Rinse Thoroughly rinse off the cleanser using lukewarm water.
  • Step 5: Pat Dry Use a clean, soft towel to gently pat your face dry.
